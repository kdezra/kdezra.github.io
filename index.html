    How do I serve my own version of 5e.tools?

    You can host a copy of 5etools on anything that can run a basic webserver—examples include your computer, your phone, a Raspberry Pi, or a free EC2/Compute Engine instance. Download the files[2024/2014] and images[2024/2014], copy them into your server's root directory, and enjoy!

    See the README or the wiki for help.

    Other versions? A "2014" version is available, as well as an archive of releases.

    FAQ

    Where's my stuff? Everything is stored in cookies. If you wipe those, or close your incognito window, it's gone forever. You can download your data for safekeeping via the Settings menu.

    Browser and device support? The latest desktop versions of Chrome and Firefox.

    Anything else I should know? Hold SHIFT when hovering to lock a window in place. Everything has tooltips. You will forever be a DM.
